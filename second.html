Violet Echoes is a creative collective formed in Amsterdam in 2003. Founders Alex Verhoeven, Sophie Langenberg, and Tomas Ruijter met at the University of Amsterdam, where they shared a passion for storytelling and visual arts. From 2003 to 2005, they collaborated on various multimedia projects under the name The Lumens before rebranding themselves as Violet Echoes to reflect their evolving artistic vision
